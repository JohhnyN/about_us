Since September 2012, the “Kazmedia Ortalygy” TV and Radio Complex, which is a 22-storey modern business technology complex, provided with infrastructure for uninterrupted production of media content and providing high-quality broadcasting, began its work in Astana. Today, Kazmedia Ortalygy has seven national television channels and three radio stations, namely Khabar, Khabar 24, KazakhTV, Qazaqstan, Balapan, Channel One Eurasia, MTRK MIR, as well as Kazakh Radio, Shalqar Radio and Astana Radio.

Creating a technology hub with the latest technology in the field of media broadcasting. Providing high-tech television and radio production for the population of the Republic of Kazakhstan.
